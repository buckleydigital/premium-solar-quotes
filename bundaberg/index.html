FAQ

Questions, answered

Is this really free? What's the catch?
Genuinely free for homeowners. We earn a referral fee from the matched installer only if you choose to go ahead, and it never changes the price you're quoted. The matching service costs you nothing, with zero obligation.
Why one installer instead of three competing quotes?
Because being hounded by five salespeople is a miserable experience. We match you exclusively with one top-rated installer for your area. They get your enquiry exclusively, so they're more invested in doing right by you. Not happy after speaking to them? Come back and we'll find an alternative.
Are the installers actually accredited?
Yes. Every installer in the network is a qualified, accredited professional. Accreditation is the standard required to install systems that qualify for government solar incentives, and it's non-negotiable for us.
Do I qualify for solar rebates?
Most owner-occupied homes are eligible for government solar incentives, but it depends on your property, system and current program rules. Your matched installer confirms exactly what you qualify for as part of the quote, so there are no surprises.
How much does solar cost?
It depends on system size, your roof and how much power you use. After government incentives a typical home system is a mid four-figure investment, but the only accurate number is a quote for your specific property, which your matched installer provides free.
How long does installation take?
Most residential solar installations are completed in a single day once paperwork and grid approval are sorted. Your matched installer walks you through the exact timeline for your property.
How soon will I hear back?
Usually within one business day. Once you're matched, your local installer reaches out directly to talk through your options and put together an obligation-free quote.
What areas do you cover?
We match by postcode. If a vetted installer covers your area, you'll go straight through. If nobody covers your postcode yet, we'll tell you upfront rather than waste your time. We're adding installers all the time.
