Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Who we are

Premium Solar Quotes (premiumsolarquotes.com.au) is a lead-matching service that connects homeowners enquiring about solar with a single pre-vetted, CEC-accredited installer covering their postcode.

What we collect

The details you enter in our quote form: your name, email, mobile number, postcode and answers about your property and power usage. We verify your mobile by SMS to confirm it's really you.

How we use it

We share your enquiry with the one installer matched to your postcode, and only with your consent given at submission. We do not sell your details to multiple companies. We retain your enquiry to operate and improve the service.

Your choices

You can ask us to access or delete your details at any time by emailing privacy@premiumsolarquotes.com.au.

This is a general summary provided for convenience and is not legal advice.
